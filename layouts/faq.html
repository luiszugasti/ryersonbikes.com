About

The Ryerson Cycling Club aims to bring all Ryersonian Cyclists together on social rides, expeditions and more, across the city and beyond. Weekly rides take place starting usually from Ryerson. Club members get to take part in the discussion on future rides and club activities.

Join our mailing list and like our Facebook page for updates. If you want Brownie points for your efforts, join our Strava club too.

The club is supervised by Luis Zugasti.
